  • Progressive Web App

    1 October 2022

    PWA stands for Progressive Web App. It's a web app that works offline, provides an installable version of the app (and not just a shortcut), and can receive push notifications. PWA apps are also able to access your camera and microphone, so you'll never be left out in the cold when it comes to using them. A key benefit of PWAs is that they work with all major browsers including Chrome, Firefox and Safari but also other mobile platforms such as Android or iOS devices where Google has made its own jump into this market with native apps like Gmail which come bundled with PWAs already built-in!
